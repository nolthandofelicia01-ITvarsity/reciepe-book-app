Cardamom Maple Salmon

Price : R109.00

Serving size : 166 calories per serving


Gluten free Paleo · Makes 1 lb of fish · Now is as good a time as any to admit my current affair with cardamom. Everyone is addicted to the sweet-savory spice that we've been incorporating it into dishes wherever possible. Here it partners with one of my favorite ingredients for glazing salmon: maple syrup. The syrup and spice make a deliciously complex coat for the fish -- the glaze strikes a chord between sweet and savory, with smoky paprika and cayenne pepper adding a hit of heat. Our chef cooked it in the oven, but for an even smokier finish, try grilling on a wood plank.
