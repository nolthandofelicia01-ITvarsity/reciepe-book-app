State Fair Shake-up

Price : R65.00

Serving size : 1 glass 30 calories


If you've ever been to the Iowa State Fair, you're familiar with its staple beverage: sweet-tart, hand-squeezed lemonade. The only possible improvement to this annual treat? Booze. To create that electric elixir with an adult edge, Wine & Dine shakes fresh-squeezed lemon juice with vodka, sugar, lemon peels and lots of ice to achieve a frothy, dangerously smooth facsimile.
