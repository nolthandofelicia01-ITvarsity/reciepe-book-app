Buffalo Wild Wings

Price : R99.00

Serving size : 8 wings 54g carbs total


A Buffalo wing, in the cuisine of the United States, is an unbreaded chicken wing section (flat or drumette) that is generally deep-fried then coated or dipped in a sauce consisting of a vinegar-based cayenne pepper hot sauce and melted butter prior to serving. There's a higher skin to meat percentage than any other part of the chicken. You may not notice but the skin of the chicken wing helps create that succulent mouth feel in a couple of bites. It's also mechanically appealing to humans because of the bone and size.
